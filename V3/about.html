About Kilroy's Pizza

Welcome to Kilroy’s Pizza, located inside Capitol Hill’s hidden gem, Cure Cocktail in the Broadway Building near Pike and Pine.
Kilroy’s Pizzas are handcrafted with love and topped with imported cheeses, housemade sauce, locally produced charcuterie, and farm-fresh herbs and veggies. We serve our own unique take on New York Style, and our distinctive version of a pan pizza. James Lorimer, a pizzaiolo with the soul of a breadmaker is also baking up some delicious loaves just for you!
